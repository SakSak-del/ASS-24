All about Fresh Feast

Mayank Chaurasia, the company's founder

Our Journey... Fresh Feast, a startup born during the pandemic, uses an online-to-offline model to distribute fresh produce. Established in 2018, Mayank Chaurasia developed VegEase to provide a seamless shopping experience for farm-fresh fruits and vegetables.

Mayank, the company's founder, says:

"We're keeping the old thela tradition alive but in a new package. Customers can enjoy handpicking fresh produce at their doorstep!"
